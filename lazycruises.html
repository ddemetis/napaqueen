Lazy Cruises

On our Lazy Cruises we give you, your family and your friends a luxurious chance to see all the Bays and Beaches of the East Coast of Cyprus in a true tranquil setting.

We will stop at the most beautiful places to swim in the crystal clear waters of the Mediterranean sea. Visiting the MAGNIFICENT VIEWS of:

  • SEA CAVES
  • KONNOS BAY
  • FIG TREE BAY (PROTARAS)
  • FAMAGUSTA COAST (GHOST TOWN)

Sunbathe on our spacious sundecks or simply relax in an interior cabin. Our boats are equiped with W.C. facilities and have full insurance and a licensed bar with friendly service.
Features:

  • Snorkelling Equipment

  • Full Facilities, toilets
